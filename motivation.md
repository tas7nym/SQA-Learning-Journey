##### Day to day Life of an SQA / Quality Engineer —

##### The role goes far beyond just finding bugs. A Quality Engineer actively participates in requirement analysis, test planning, test design, automation, and collaboration with developers and product managers. What resonated with me most is that SQA engineers influence the product from the very beginning of the development lifecycle, not just at the end. This proactive involvement makes the role impactful and intellectually engaging.

##### Why SQA Is Important in 2026 —

##### With the rise of AI-driven systems, cloud platforms, fintech applications, and large-scale user bases, even a small defect can lead to financial loss, security breaches, or reputational damage. Quality Engineers in 2026 are expected to focus not only on functional testing, but also on automation, performance, security, and reliability.This evolution of quality engineering is no longer a support function , it has become a core pillar of software engineering and business success.
