A real world micro-STLC project simulation : - 

simplified STLC  Execution on open cart  e-commerce platform : -

Phase 1:- Planning & Analysis
Phase 2 :- Design & Execution
3 functional requirement analysis of ’’open-cart’’ - , Design & Execution : -
Account creation and sign-in  ,  adding item and update  ,  check out 
 		
Use case design —

1.
Use Case - Account creation and sign-in
Goal -   visitor issues creating account and sign in
Failed End Condition - fail to create and fail to sign-in
Primary Actors - visitor and buyer
Secondary Actors - development team 
Trigger - Account creation and sign-in request come but fail to execute
Main Success Scenario - 1. Visitor calls for creating account request
				1.1. Visitor calls in via pc
				1.2. Visitor give valid information
				1.3. Visitor submit
			    2. Website didn’t receive visitor information
Quality Requirements  - 1.invalid information shows as notification
			  2. Shows failing confirmation


2.
Use Case - adding item and update
Goal -  visitor issues adding and updating product to cart 
Success End Condition - successfully adding and updating item
Primary Actors - visitor and buyer
Secondary Actors - item investor ,website owner , server
Main Success Scenario - 1. Call for item availability check
				1.1. Shows available item
			   2. Enter item amount
			   3. Adding item to cart
			   4. View item to cart
				4.1. Viewing item
				4.2. Chose to change item amount
				4.3. Shows updated item
Alternative Flows - 1. Fail to adding
			1.1. Item shows
			1.2. Notify 1015 error
Quality Requirements  - 1. Buyer must check out within stock limit



3.
Use Case - check out 
Goal - buyer issues check out to the company website and expect good shipped and to be billed.
Success End Condition - successfully check out with details and shows invoice
Primary Actors - buyer
Secondary Actors - bank , item investor , website company
Main Success Scenario - 1. Buyer call for check out
   2. Company receive buyer details
   3. Bank clarify buyer transition  
Quality Requirements  -  1. Buyer must complete check out with stock availability
				1.1. Buyer must complete transaction with in 3 minute 
			 2. Company must keep record of customer record for further warranty 
and return policy 
			3. Bank must clarify and give confirmation massage both customer and 
Company via mail and massage

Phase 3 :- Closure
